Dr. Karlene Guasteferro, DDS, PC

Dr. Karlene Guastefferro began her Dental Career at the Ohio State University college of Dentistry in 1986. Graduating as a Doctor of Dental Surgery in 1991, Dr. Guastefferro began serving the community of Smyrna Tennessee for 3 plus years and from 1992 - 1995 the residents of Smyrna enjoyed the services of Dr. Guasteferro for their dental needs. From there Dr. Guasteferro moved back home to the state of New York and joined the Dental group of Barzman, Kasimor and Vieth, where she was able to expand her expertise as well as the care she was able to provide to her patients. While at Barzman, Kasimor and Vieth  from 1994 - 2001 Dr. Guastefferro was involved with Restorative Dentistry, Cosmetic Dentistry, and Endodontic Dentistry. Staying in New York Dr. Guasteferro co-owned Northstar Dental and from 2001 - 2006 provided dental care to West Seneca New York. After being a co-owner at Northstar Dr. Guasteferro moved on to work with Dr. Barry Socket as an associate General Dentist from 2007 - 2011. From there Dr. Guasteferro moved on to very challenging yet infinitely rewarding positions as a General Dentist for Titusville Area Hospital Tionesta Dental and as a General Dentist/Independent Contractor while holding  multiple licenses in the states of NY, WI, PA, and TN. Dr. Guasteferro held these positions from 2011 - 2014 and 2014 - 2016 respectively.

Dr. Guasteferro has a long and selfless track record of using her expertise as a General dentist for communities and organizations like “Give Kids a Smile”; an organization that Introduces children to dentistry, screening and cleaning with oral hygiene instructions. Along with her Charitable Pursuits Dr. Guasteferro has numerous professional awards as well, belonging to organizations such as Delta Sigma Delta Dental Fraternity Mu Mu Chapter where she was the Social Director. Dr. Guasteferro also belongs to the academy of General Dentistry as well as the American Dental Association.

Currently Dr. Guastefferro has partnered with Smile Direct Club as it seeks to make orthodontic care available to the masses by maintaining quality of care while at the same time making it affordable and accessible to everyone.
