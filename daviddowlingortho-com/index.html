Dr. David Barclay Dowling, DDS, PC

Dr. David Dowling has been helping patients in the orthodontic field for over 35 years. A Native of Baton Rouge Louisiana, Dr. Dowling has had the opportunity to travel the United States and live in many areas while serving his patient population. From 1964 - 1968 Dr. Dowling even spent his time pitching his way around the United States as a member of the St. Louis Cardinals and Chicago Cubs baseball teams.

Dr. David Dowling began his professional orthodontic career in Private orthodontic practice in Longview WA. Graduating from The University of TN Dental School in 1971 with his DDS, and Northwestern University in 1973 with his MS in Orthodontics; Dr. Dowling spent his time faithfully serving his patients, the community, and family alike in Longview for 23 years, from 1973 - 1996. From there Dr. Dowling moved to Tucson and Phoenix AZ to open his orthodontics practice and served those communities faithfully from 1996 - 2000 and from 2000 - 2009 respectively. After serving in private practice for many years Dr. Dowling Joined Western Dental Orthodontics, with locations in CA and NV as a corporate Consultant from 2010 - 2015.

Over the years Dr. Dowling has been recognized with honors and invited to be member of prestigious organizations such as OKU, American Dental Association, American Association of Orthodontics, Pacific Coast Society of Orthodontics, Diplomate American Board of Orthodontics, and the Angle Society.

In his latest adventure, Dr. Dowling has partnered with Smile Direct Club, in an effort to bring the life changing promise of a beautiful smile to both a national and world wide patient base; all the while making it affordable to all who want a radiant smile. Dr. Dowling has been partnered with Smile Direct Club since February of 2016.
